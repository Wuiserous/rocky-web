## Style Prompt
Create a cozy premium desktop demo for Rocky, a pixel-art AI pet that lives on the user's screen. The video should feel cinematic, polished, warm, and natural: a dark desktop workspace with soft window glass, readable assistant panels, glowing task states, and Rocky moving between actions. The charm comes from the real pet animations, not generic tech gradients.

## Colors
- `#070b0a` deep desktop background
- `#fff6dc` warm cream text
- `#d9ff7d` Rocky action highlight
- `#77d8ff` web/search highlight
- `#f4c86b` news/file accent
- `rgba(255,255,255,0.12)` thin glass borders

## Typography
Use Segoe UI Variable / Inter / system sans-serif. Headlines are heavy and rounded; UI labels are compact, uppercase, and high-contrast.

## What NOT to Do
- Do not make it feel like a slideshow or explainer deck.
- Do not hide Rocky under UI panels.
- Do not use generic neon blue SaaS styling.
- Do not use large empty cards without a visible task.
- Do not create abrupt jump cuts; the whole demo should feel like one continuous desktop moment.
